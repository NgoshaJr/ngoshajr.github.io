[The Open Virtual Lab (OVL)]() is a web-based platform designed to assist earth observation scientists. The website will provide virtual practicals using [R](https://)  packages and [Python](https://)  libraries to acquire, process, analyze, and visualize [satellite remotely sensed](https://)  data and [in situ](https://)  data from [CTD](https://), [buoys](https://) , and [Argo floats](https://). The acquired datasets will help assess and evaluate the [spatial](https://)  and [temporal](https://) variations of patterns from the [ocean](https://) , [land](https://), and [atmosphere](https://). As a result,  can improve knowledge and raise awareness about the [conservation](https://) and [monitoring](https://) of natural resources (e.g., marine and coastal resources). Similarly, the platform will offer different approaches for [geospatial data analytics](https://) using [Machine Learning](https://) and [Artificial Intelligence (AI)](https://) algorithms.
