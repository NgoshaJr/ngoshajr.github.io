`The Open Virtual Lab` (OVL) is a web-based platform designed to assist earth observation scientists. The website will provide virtual practicals using `R` packages and `Python` libraries to acquire, process, analyze, and visualize `satellite remotely sensed` data and `in situ` data from `CTDs`, `buoys`, and `Argo floats`. The acquired datasets will help assess and evaluate the spatial and temporal variations of patterns from the `ocean`, `land`, and `atmosphere`. As a result,  can improve knowledge and raise awareness about the `conservation` and `monitoring` of natural resources (e.g., marine and coastal resources). Similarly, the platform will offer different approaches for geospatial data analytics using Machine Learning and Artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms.
