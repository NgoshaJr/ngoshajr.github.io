The study was focused on revealing the potential of an artificial deep neural network to detect two seagrass species, Enhalus acoroides and Halophila ovalis. The research project aimed to develop a seagrass detection model from UAV data. The study explores the potential of artificial deep neural networks and unnamed aerial vehicle (UAV) imagery.
