There are many scientific data servers available in the world that provide satellite data with their own formatting, downloading, and subsetting protocals. However, this may pose a big challenge for biginners to choose a consistent and reliable server. By Using erddap server may fills the gap and unify the advantages in a single service. Alaso, erddap supports different file formats such as html table, ESRI .asc and .csv, Google Earth .kml, OPeNDAP binary, .mat, .nc, ODV .txt, .csv, .tsv, .json, and .xhtml, and even some image formats (.png and .pdf).
